White Birch Lodge

All units at White Birch Lodge are reserved exclusively for our wedding guests. The property is lakefront and includes private beaches, docks, kayaks, paddleboards, fire pits, and shared outdoor spaces.

Accommodation Types

  • Lodge rooms (close to reception)
  • Cabins (rustic, beachy)
  • Condos & townhouses (ideal for families and groups)
  • Conrad House (quieter, overlooking the lake)

Rates & Booking Notes

  • Nightly rates generally range from $265–$575
  • Two-night minimum stay required
  • 6% lodging tax applies
  • Additional guest fees may apply
  • AC may be added ($150) to many units; Reserve early - Limited #'s
